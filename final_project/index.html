Inspiration

I decided to make my website bright colors so that it is interesting to look at. I'm a fan of aesthics and so I wanted to make something that would be appealing to people.

Why blue & purple?

I chose blue and purple because they go together well and it will catch your attention. Its a good representation of the modern aesthetic. It stands out and it's also very bold.

What makes something aesthetic?

Something that is pleasing to you, for example a photo or sculpture. It's when you appreciate the beauty in a photo or a object.
